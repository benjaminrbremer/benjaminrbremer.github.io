An Analysis of an Intellectual Property Rights Based Subsidy

We identify a relatively novel type of subsidy that aims to bring privately optimal levels of R&D into balance with social optimum, and compare it to tradition input and output based subsidies.

Overview

This concept was originally a section in my first paper, The Economics of AI R&D in Waste Management Applications. After we dug more into the topic, and after some feedback from reviewers, we decided that this concept could be turned into its own paper. It is still in the early stages, as we are trying to get the first paper accepted before moving on to a second.
