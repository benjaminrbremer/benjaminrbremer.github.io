Ethical Analysis of John Deere

Analyzing the ethics of John Deere's actions as they relate to the recent right to repair lawsuits.

Overview

This paper was written in Fall 2025 for my business ethics class. The goal was to work in a team over the course of the semester to analyze the business ethics of recent actions taken by a public US company. My team chose to analyze John Deere and their actions relating to the recent right to repair lawsuits.

My role included the economic analysis of their actions, as well as helping team members research and edit for their sections.
